### **STEP 3: Z-SCORE DIVERGENCE CALCULATION**

**Action:** Calculate Z-score for each metal manually

**Formula:**

```
Z-Score = (Current Price - 20-period SMA) / Standard Deviation(20)
```

**Example for Gold:**

- 20-period SMA: $2,650 (read from chart)
- Standard Deviation(20): $12 (read from indicator)
- Z-Score = (2680 - 2650) / 12 = **+2.5**

**Interpretation:**

- **Positive Z-score:** Metal is trading above its 20-period average (overbought)
- **Negative Z-score:** Metal is trading below its 20-period average (oversold)
- **Magnitude matters:** +2.5 is more extreme than +1.0

**Repeat for Silver and Platinum.**

**Decision:**

1. Identify **Leader** (highest Z-score)
2. Identify **Laggard** (lowest/most negative Z-score)
3. Calculate **Spread** = Leader Z-score - Laggard Z-score

**Entry requirements:**

- Leader must have absolute Z-score **> +2.0**
